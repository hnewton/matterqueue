## Idea Capsule — 2026-06-16

**Name** — Groundtruth

**One Phrase** — *Facts, Without the Fight* *(uncertain)*

**Core Sentence** — A nonprofit open dataset commons that makes it easy for anyone to find, share, and visualize factual data on contested topics — grounding public discourse in reality without becoming another partisan fact-checking operation

**One Paragraph** — Public arguments have stopped sharing a reality, and most fact-checking just becomes another partisan operation. Groundtruth is a nonprofit dataset commons that makes it easy to find, share, and visualize factual data on contested topics — not who's right, just what's true. The discipline of staying apolitical is the entire value and the entire challenge, which is why it's built as a coalition of credible institutions rather than a startup, with a consumer fact-checking extension layered on top. In an era of collapsing trust, shared data infrastructure is exactly the thing nobody owns and everybody needs.

**Key Insights**
- Apolitical framing is the entire value proposition and the entire design challenge — discipline to maintain data-first neutrality is harder than building the technology
- Dataset commons as foundation; Chrome extension as consumer application built on top — separate products, separate risk profiles
- Coalition model not a startup — convening credible institutions around shared infrastructure is more powerful than building alone
- Being right isn't enough anymore — presentation, sourcing, governance, and partnerships all have to signal trustworthiness before anyone reads a word
- Real-time fact-checking extension is technically feasible now

**User** — Citizens wanting to ground conversations in facts; journalists, educators, researchers; policy makers; Democracy Docket, Constitution Center, Poynter, Pulitzer as founding partners

**Model** — Nonprofit or open source; grants (democracy, civic tech, journalism foundations); institutional data partnerships; Chrome extension as free consumer product

**Why It's Real**
- Demand for trustworthy shared data infrastructure at all-time high
- Named institutions give immediate credibility a startup can't buy
- Open source governance is the best available answer to neutrality credibility problem
- DataMutants provides the data science backbone

**Flags** — Neutrality is genuinely hard to maintain at scale; trust in institutions at historic low; Chrome extension has different political risk profile than dataset library — sequence carefully; fact-checking sustainability is hard

**Synergy**
- DataMutants (data science delivery partner)
- Playfield (shared dataset commons powers simulations)
- Commonground (shared factual infrastructure underneath empathy layer)
- Obviousli (Groundtruth data as explainer source)
