## Idea Capsule — 2026-06-16

**Name** — Estatecoach

**One Phrase** — *Estate Planning Without the Dread* *(uncertain)*

**Core Sentence** — An AI-guided estate planning coach that starts with the person not the documents — educating, demystifying, and preparing anyone to take the next step with confidence, regardless of wealth or complexity

**One Paragraph** — You don't need to be rich to need an estate plan, but the 'you have to be wealthy' myth kills action before anyone opens a document. Estatecoach starts with the person, not the paperwork — an AI-guided coach that educates and demystifies until you're genuinely ready to take the next step, whatever your wealth or complexity. LegalZoom sells documents and Nolo sells content; nobody has built the coached, conversational, education-first experience, and prepared clients are worth real referral fees to the attorneys who get them. The market is enormous, the barrier is psychological, and conversation is exactly what dissolves it.

**Key Insights**
- Barrier is entirely psychological not financial — "you have to be rich" myth kills action before anyone opens a document
- InsightMutant question-asking experience is the right interaction model — conversation first, documents emerge from understanding
- Attorney referral is potentially the real business — qualified, educated, prepared clients are worth significant lead fees; both sides win
- LegalZoom and Rocket Lawyer are document companies; Nolo is a content company; nobody has built the coached conversational education-first experience
- Jurisdictional variation is the core technical challenge — clean architecture for state-level variation is non-negotiable from day one
- Honey Copp content partnership is a meaningful head start
- Not-legal-advice guardrail is identical to Subtext — both products share this positioning

**User** — Adults who know they should have an estate plan and have been avoiding it; newly married couples, new parents, people who just received an inheritance; estate attorneys; financial advisors, HR benefits programs

**Model** — Freemium consumer; attorney referral marketplace (qualified lead fees); financial advisor and insurance agent white label; HR benefits integration; content partnership model

**Why It's Real**
- Market is enormous — majority of American adults have no estate plan
- Psychological barrier is addressable through conversational AI in ways static documents never could be
- Attorney referral model has proven revenue in adjacent legal markets
- HR benefits angle provides B2B revenue that smooths consumer acquisition costs

**Flags** — Not providing legal advice is a critical and non-negotiable guardrail; keeping content current across 50 states is significant ongoing operational cost; competition from LegalZoom, Rocket Lawyer, Trust & Will; state regulation of legal services requires careful legal architecture; unauthorized practice of law exposure needs legal counsel; connection to The Current is optional not architectural

**Synergy**
- InsightMutant (primary engine powering the coaching conversation)
- Pebble (estate giving, charitable bequests as an Estatecoach module)
- Phase Next (estate planning is a defining 50-something life task)
- Selfont (handwritten legacy letters as Estatecoach output; formerly Inkself)
- Uncard (memorial card planning as estate preparation component)
- Subtext (estate planning documents rendered as plain English audit; shared not-legal-advice guardrail)
