## Idea Capsule — 2026-06-16

**Name** — InsightMutant

**One Phrase** — [FILL: one phrase — the sharpest distillation]

**Core Sentence** — A question-asking engine AND a product that surfaces what someone doesn't know they need to say — replacing surveys and questionnaires with a qualitative conversational experience for onboarding, exit interviews, usage discovery, and any domain requiring structured human understanding

**One Paragraph** — Most AI tools rush to answer; InsightMutant asks. It's a question-asking engine that surfaces what someone doesn't know they need to say, replacing the dead survey and the awkward intake form with a conversation that actually goes somewhere — and the same engine that runs an estate-planning intake can run an exit interview, a photoshoot plan, or an executive-function check-in, then hand back structured insight shaped to the moment. It's already across multiple deep build sessions, and it's the rare asset that's both core infrastructure and a product people would pay for on its own. When the questions are this good, the answers take care of themselves.

**Key Insights**
- The question is the product — not the answer; most AI tools answer, InsightMutant asks; that inversion is the entire value
- Domain-agnostic engine with domain-specific deployments — same questioning engine powers estate planning onboarding, photoshoot planning, talent assessment, executive function coaching
- Qualitative experience layer — replaces surveys and questionnaires with conversational depth; that's a standalone product market not just infrastructure
- Schema on read output — structured insight generated from conversation rendered as plan, brief, profile, or recommendation depending on context
- Socratic method at scale — surfaces assumptions, gaps, and contradictions the person couldn't surface themselves
