## Idea Capsule — 2026-06-16

**Name** — Pawpath

**One Phrase** — *Walk a Mile in Their Paws* *(uncertain)*

**Core Sentence** — A top-down 2D dog walking game where your dog's defined personality drives every encounter on the grid — charming, educational, and built around real shelter dog profiles to drive adoption awareness

**One Paragraph** — Every dog sees the world differently, and Pawpath lets you live it — a top-down walking game where your dog's personality drives every encounter, so an anxious rescue greyhound navigates the block nothing like a fearless golden puppy. That variance is the replayability and the emotional attachment, and real shelter-dog profiles give it a cause, a content pipeline, and a distribution partner at once. The Tamagotchi attachment model is one of the most proven mechanics in games. Charming, buildable, and connected to adoption from day one.

**Key Insights**
- Personality-first mechanic is the hook — an anxious rescue greyhound navigates the world completely differently than a fearless golden retriever puppy; variance drives replayability and emotional attachment
- What the dog encounters is the content engine — squirrels, puddles, strangers, cats, skateboarders, other dogs — each personality-dependent
- Shelter dog profile integration is the soul — real dogs, real backstories, real adoption pipeline
- Tamagotchi attachment model is proven and deep
- Top-down 2D grid is the right scope — approachable to build, readable to play
- Cognitive angle is quiet but real — spatial navigation, consequence anticipation, attention management

**User** — Kids and dog lovers of any age; prospective dog adopters; cognitive maintenance users; humane societies, pet brands

**Model** — Free app with optional donation mechanic; sponsorship (pet industry); community development of dog breeds and personality types; humane society partnerships

**Why It's Real**
- Tamagotchi and virtual pet attachment is one of the most proven mechanics in gaming history
- Shelter dog profile angle gives it a cause, a content pipeline, and a distribution partner simultaneously
- Top-down 2D is achievable as a small team or solo build
- Sponsorship surface is large and directly brand-relevant

**Flags** — Emotional attachment mechanic cuts both ways; personality system complexity can spiral — 5-6 well-designed types beats 20 shallow ones; resist multiplayer until core loop is proven; **PawPath domain owned by Zoetis** — naming conflict is a hard blocker before any public release; replacement name not yet identified; prototype was informative but name must resolve before investment increases; Project Genie (Google Maps and Street View integration) noted as a potential direction during prototype session

**Synergy**
- Playfield (shelter adoption awareness as social systems simulation)
- Patterntopia (ambient scene contexts include natural environments — following Flowscape absorption)
- Uncard (dog-themed anti-greeting cards)
- Walkframe (physical walking club for dog owners)
