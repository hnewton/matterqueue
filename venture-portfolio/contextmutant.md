## Idea Capsule — 2026-06-16

**Name** — ContextMutant

**One Phrase** — [FILL: one phrase — the sharpest distillation]

**Core Sentence** — An AI fluency coaching and measurement layer that helps people — and their employers — understand how effectively they're using AI, track improvement over time, and develop the context discipline and prompt literacy that separates 20% AI users from 80% AI users

**One Paragraph** — Most people use AI at a fraction of its capability — not for lack of intelligence but for lack of fluency, and fluency is teachable and measurable, which makes it a product. ContextMutant measures how effectively a person or team actually uses AI, then closes the gap with specific interventions and a before/after score employers can trust. The differentiator is the data source: real conversation history via Trail System, not a quiz — what context people bring and how output quality tracks input quality. For organizations pouring money into AI tooling, this is the missing answer to whether any of it is landing.

**Key Insights**
- Most people use AI at 20% of its capability — not because they lack intelligence but because they lack fluency; fluency is teachable and measurable, which makes it a product
- Employer B2B is the commercial angle — organizations investing in AI tooling want to know if their teams are actually getting value from it; measurable fluency improvement is that answer
- Trail System is the data source — conversation history is the evidence base for fluency assessment; what context people bring, how they structure prompts, how output quality correlates with input quality
- Measurable improvement loop is the product — not abstract coaching but a before/after fluency score with specific interventions that move it; accountability through measurement
- PromptMutant absorbed — prompt literacy is one component of fluency, not a separate product; reusable prompt patterns live here as a fluency tool not a standalone library
