StyleVault is not a standalone app. It is a native capture and retrieval surface within The Current — specifically the creative and aesthetic attention layer. The prompting aspect (style prompts, reusable prompt patterns) has a secondary PLG path into ContextMutant. What you save, pin, and return to in styles, visual references, and prompts is the same behavioral taste signal The Current already captures across notes, bookmarks, and conversations. The moodboard/pinboard output is a schema on read applied to the style stream — same corpus, different rendering. Creative identity as an output rather than knowledge graph. StyleVault also belongs naturally to FrameLab and any visually-driven creative process. Pre-production moodboarding for photography shoots, video projects, architectural walkthroughs, garden planning, and other field-based visual activities is a native StyleVault use case — not just The Current's capture layer but a planning surface for any domain where visual reference gathering precedes the work.
