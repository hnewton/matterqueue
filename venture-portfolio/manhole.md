## Idea Capsule — 2026-06-16

**Name** — Manhole

**One Phrase** — *Cities, Underfoot* *(uncertain)*

**Core Sentence** — Turn photos of manhole covers and urban surface designs into physical collectibles, building a global community archive of cities through their street-level art

**One Paragraph** — Manhole covers are quietly, obsessively collected — and Manhole turns a photo of one into a physical collectible you can actually hold, with the provenance of having found it yourself. The photo-to-relief pipeline is the reusable engine; the cover is just the first surface, and every submission makes a global community archive of cities-through-their-street-art richer. It's the validated first vertical of ShapeSake, proving the pipeline works before scaling to every passion community. You found it, you photographed it, now you own it.

**Key Insights**
- Manhole covers are already obsessively collected by a small passionate community — distribution is findable
- Photo-to-relief pipeline (not full photogrammetry) is the reusable engine — manhole is just the first surface
- Physical collectible per city is a souvenir with personal provenance — you found it, you made it
- Community archive compounds like Foldex — every submission makes the library richer
- Travel write-off case is legitimate and direct
- The photo-to-relief engine could power other apps; manhole cover is the initial surface

**User** — Travelers with aesthetic curiosity, urban explorers, design-obsessed collectors, city lovers

**Model** — Per-collectible commerce (print/cast on demand); community archive with contribution mechanic; potential licensing of photo-to-relief engine

**Why It's Real**
- You already have the collection and the habit — you're the first user
- Physical output is the business model, not an afterthought
- Niche community with real passion already exists
- Engine has broader application value beyond the initial surface

**Flags** — Photo-to-relief accuracy needs validation; physical casting adds fulfillment complexity; manhole cover IP is murky in some jurisdictions

**Synergy**
- ShapeSake — Manhole is an early vertical of the ShapeSake capability; the photo-to-relief pipeline proven in the Manhole prototype is the same pipeline ShapeSake uses for passion community physical reproduction; manhole covers are one surface type among many that ShapeSake can produce; the Manhole prototype session also produced details useful for Siteframe's photo-to-3D room engine and the broader photo-to-object pipeline
- Patterntopia, Foldex (World Surfaces cluster, shared physical output instinct)
- Siteframe (shared computer vision infrastructure; Manhole prototype conversation contains relevant details for Siteframe photo-to-3D room engine)
- Whenivia — urban surface collectors are a natural Whenivia affinity domain; "when was this manhole cover design introduced?" is exactly the Whenivia mechanic
