## Idea Capsule — 2026-06-16

**Name** — Persona

**One Phrase** — *Every AI, a Specific Mind* *(uncertain)*

**Core Sentence** — A marketplace of swappable AI personality modules that make any AI experience feel like a specific mind — turning interview footage, writing, and conversation into character layers that sit on top of any LLM without retraining it

**One Paragraph** — The knowledge is the LLM; the character is Persona. It's a marketplace of swappable personality modules that make any AI experience feel like a specific mind — Feynman's curiosity, a particular coach, a fictional guide — sitting on top of any model without retraining it. As LLMs commoditize, personality becomes the differentiation, and an interview-to-module pipeline lets non-technical creators build characters from source material into a real creator economy. EF Coach and InsightMutant are immediate internal customers; you choose who you're talking to.

**Key Insights**
- Personality is a layer not a model — Feynman's curiosity applied to any AI experience; knowledge is the LLM underneath, character is the module on top; no retraining required
- Interview-to-training-data pipeline is the technical unlock — corpus of lectures, interviews, and writing is sufficient to extract a question-asking pattern and communication style
- Swappable modules create a creative economy — character designers build, experience builders license, users choose
- "AI to build AI" is the accessibility layer — non-technical creators use AI to build personality modules from source material
- Fictional characters are a rich and underexplored surface — original platform characters, public domain figures, licensed estate partnerships
- No dead ends is the right design principle

**User** — Experience builders; educators wanting historical or fictional figures as learning guides; consumers wanting preferred AI personality across tools; edtech platforms, corporate training, entertainment

**Model** — Marketplace (creator royalties, platform percentage); subscription for experience builders; licensed character partnerships (estate and publisher deals); white label for enterprise; consumer tier

**Why It's Real**
- Personality layer without retraining is technically achievable now
- The generic AI problem is real and growing — as LLMs commoditize, personality becomes primary differentiation
- Educational market has clear appetite — historical figure simulations being attempted badly everywhere
- InsightMutant and EF Coach are immediate internal customers

**Flags** — Copyright and likeness rights for real people — needs clear legal framework; quality variance in community-built modules could damage trust; personality without knowledge is frustrating if not designed carefully

**Synergy**
- InsightMutant (Persona modules make question-asking feel like a specific mind)
- EF Coach (personality layer makes coaching feel like a relationship)
- Phase Next (historical mentor figures as learning guides)
- Framelab (editor personality modules for shoot feedback)
- Estatecoach (warm trustworthy personality makes intimidating topic approachable)
